# REFERENCES.md — Books and resources for learning Git

## Books

- **Pro Git (2nd Edition)** by Scott Chacon & Ben Straub — free at https://git-scm.com/book
- **Version Control with Git** by Jon Loeliger & Matthew McCullough — O'Reilly
- **Git Pocket Guide** by Richard E. Silverman — O'Reilly

## Online

- **Git Reference Manual** — https://git-scm.com/docs
- **Git Internals** — Chapter 10 of Pro Git (objects, refs, packfiles)
- **Oh Shit, Git!?!** — https://ohshitgit.com/ (common recovery scenarios)
- **Git from the Bottom Up** by John Wiegley — https://jwiegley.github.io/git-from-the-bottom-up/
